NOODLES

Description

it is a chinese food easy and take less time to cook

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • vegetable
  • oil
  • water
  • salt
  • magic masala

Procedures

  1. boil the water 2 minutes
  2. add oil and noodles in water for 10 minutes
  3. filter the water from noodles
  4. dry the filtered noodles
  5. saute the vegetables with magic masala
  6. put the noodles in kadai and mix well
  7. then serve it on plate
