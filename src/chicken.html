CHICKEN

Description

chicken is the non veg item every body like it and good for health

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • neccesary vegetables
  • masalas
  • ghee
  • oil

Procedures

  1. wash the chicken in tap water
  2. boil the chicken with oil
  3. saute vegetables into the kadai
  4. add the masala items
  5. put the boiled chicken into it
  6. add water and boil 15 minutes
  7. serve the chicken in plate
