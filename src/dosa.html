DOSA

Description

Dosa is the fantastic and simple dish with rice flour

Ingredient

  • rice flour
  • oil
  • salt

Procedure

  1. take the rice flour
  2. mix it well
  3. pour it on pan
  4. spread the flour on the pan well
  5. put oil on pan
  6. flip the dosa
  7. finally take way from pan and put on plate
